Mapping FEMA Declared Disasters

The Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (Stafford Act) provides natural disaster federal assistance for state and local governments in carrying out their responsibilities to aid citizens. A presidential disaster declaration or an emergency declaration triggers financial and physical assistance through the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA).
